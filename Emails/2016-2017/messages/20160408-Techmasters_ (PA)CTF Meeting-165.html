This Sunday at 2:30pm, come to the Makerspace to learn about online cybersecurity competitions! We will be introducing CTFs, short for "Capture the Flags," which are a type of computer security competition where “you hack, decrypt, reverse, and do whatever it takes to” solve problems and capture secret answer strings called "flags." We will go over some problems from past competitions, and walk through the steps to solve them.

In case you missed the announcement a few weeks ago, PACTF, the first CTF competition by PA, is starting this Sunday. You should sign up whether you consider yourself a "beginner" or an "expert." Over $3,000 worth of prizes will be awarded to the top-ranking teams!
