Hello Techmasters,

Excited for the first night of blitzes? Here are a few important things:
  • Be sure to read the online Techmasters FAQ thoroughly. The online version (not the PDF) has new information that solves new problems. It is here: https://techmasters.io/techguide
  • Wear your Techmasters shirts during the Blitzes. You may wear this year's shirt or last year's. We have multiple days of Blitzes so plan accordingly, whether to wear your shirt less during the day, try to keep them clean, and/or do more laundry.
  • Let new students (and others) know where to find tech help. We are (almost) always available at techmasters.io.
  • Let your house counselors know that you will not be able to return for sign-in/dorm meeting on time. We will definitely be done by 10:30 p.m., maybe earlier. They should be notified about your Techmasters commitment at some point tomorrow, but you should also let them know directly. Techmasters Dorm Blitzes are listed on the official opening of school schedule, available on PAnet and in the email from Ms. Korn.
  • We meet at 7:50 p.m. at the Main St. / Chapel Ave. intersection, on the Graves side of the road. Here is a campus map with all the dorms: https://goo.gl/WmtTTa
If you are part of the Techmasters work duty, or are otherwise enrolled onto our online support chat system (tawk.to), please be sure to accept the invitation (if prompted in an email) and install the app on your phone so you can be notified and respond to live support requests during your free time. There is a "push notification" feature that should be enabled.
