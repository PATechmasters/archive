In lieu of our usual meeting, we invite you to come to the second floor of the library on Sunday from 1–4 p.m. for a Wikipedia Edit-a-thon, hosted by the Brace Center, the Archives, and the OWH Library.

No Wikipedia editing experience is necessary. Friendly volunteers will be on hand to help answer your editing questions and provide training and research materials. Everyone is welcome. Refreshments will be provided, and we were told that “the event will culminate in a festive reception.”

If you are interested, please email Flavia Vidal (fvidal@andover.edu) or Paige Roberts (proberts@andover.edu) so that you can be included in editing groups. Alternatively, you can register on the event page if you already edit Wikipedia. Be sure to bring a laptop with you to the event!
