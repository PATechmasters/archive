Hey Techmasters,

We will be having a meeting tomorrow (Sunday, December 11) in the Makerspace at 2pm.

The bulk of the meeting will be devoted to learning how to solve TMCTF’s most difficult problem, Make-A-Face. This problem requires working knowledge of Perl, HTML, CSS, the UNIX filesystem, Javascript, HTTP parameters, and the way data is encoded. You’ll also learn how to use Postman and Hex Works. It’s a lot to cover, but by the end of the meeting, you will know how to infiltrate the Make-A-Face server’s filesystem, extract the flag, and solve the problem.

We will start from square one for this problem, and assume no prior technical knowledge. That means that if you haven’t been coming to Techmasters, this is the perfect time to jump in. Like always, it will be a terabyte of fun.

Bring your laptops and bring your brains, because this hacking crash-course will be fast paced.

See you tomorrow,
