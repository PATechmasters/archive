Last meeting, while we were busy looking at poorly drawn diagrams of a CPU's guts and fiddling with screwdrivers and wires and pointy things that may or may not still work, I was struck by a strange thought. What is a computer?


You may think that's a silly question, coming from the glorious dictator-for-life-but-not-really-i-wish of the Masters of Technology, but it's a good one. Anybody can tell you that a computer is this magical box (or, at this point, even just a random plastic plate) that you can use to surf the internet and play League of Legends, but beyond that is a little harder. How exactly does a computer do the things it does? Want to find out?
