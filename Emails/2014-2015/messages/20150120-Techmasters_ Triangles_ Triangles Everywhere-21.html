This week we'll be talking about something very simple yet very important: TRIANGLES
"What is a triangle", you ask?  Actually, I sincerely hope you don't ask that.  What you should ask, however, is "Why do we care about triangles?"

If any of you play video games (so basically all of you), you may already know the answer.  Triangles are the fundamental building block of computer graphics.
Everything on your screen is a triangle, unless you're on Google Plus; I heard they were trying to do stuff with circles and it didn't go so well.

The floor in a game? Triangles.
Your rectangular Firefox window? Two triangles (actually though).
Your Vim Emacs window? Triangles.
Your Internet Explorer window?

Sorry about that.
